# Customer Data Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer shopping behavior using Power BI.  
The analysis covers key dimensions such as **gender, age groups, product categories, and payment methods** to identify sales patterns, revenue contribution, and actionable business insights.

The dataset represents customer transactions from **10 shopping malls in Istanbul** between **2021 and 2023**, providing a comprehensive view of customer purchasing habits.

## Objectives
- Analyze shopping distribution by gender and age group
- Compare product sales and revenue across demographics
- Understand customer preferences by product category
- Study payment method usage and its relation to customer attributes
- Generate meaningful insights to support business decision-making

## Dataset Description
The dataset includes the following attributes:
- Invoice Number
- Customer ID
- Gender
- Age
- Product Category
- Quantity
- Price
- Payment Method (Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card)
- Invoice Date
- Shopping Mall Location

## Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop
- Microsoft Excel

## Dashboard Insights
- Female customers contribute the highest shopping activity and revenue
- The **46–60 age group** is the most active and highest revenue-generating segment
- Categories like **Clothing, Cosmetics, and Books** are popular across all age groups
- Cash remains the most used payment method, especially among older customers
- Younger and middle-aged customers prefer digital payment methods

## How to Use
1. Download the `.pbix` file from this repository
2. Open it using **Power BI Desktop**
3. Interact with filters and visuals to explore customer insights
